Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your first appointment.

Do you accept international patients?
Yes, we welcome patients from all over the world. We offer virtual consultations, multilingual support, and can coordinate with your local dentist for records transfer and follow-up care.
How long does a dental implant procedure take?
The implant placement itself takes 1–2 hours. The full process including osseointegration (bone fusion) typically spans 3–6 months, after which the crown is fitted. We schedule all stages to minimise your travel time.
Is teeth whitening safe for sensitive teeth?
Absolutely. We use a pre-treatment assessment to evaluate sensitivity levels and select the appropriate whitening concentration. Most patients experience minimal to no sensitivity with our professional-grade systems.
Do you offer payment plans or finance?
Yes. We offer 0% interest finance on treatments over $500, spread over 6–24 months. We accept major credit cards, bank transfers, and most international payment methods.
Can I get a virtual consultation before visiting?
Yes, we offer free 20-minute video consultations with our specialists. Simply upload recent photos or X-rays and our team will provide a detailed preliminary assessment and treatment quote before you travel.
What makes your clinic stand out from others?
Our combination of internationally certified specialists, cutting-edge technology (3D imaging, laser dentistry), and a dedicated international patient coordinator makes us the trusted choice for dental tourists worldwide.
